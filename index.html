Create a Relaxing Bedtime Routine

Warm Bath or Shower

A warm bath or shower before bed helps relax muscles and Prepare your body for sleep.
The warmth can help to reduce stress and promote drowsiness.

Reading or Meditation

Instead of scrolling through social media, engage in relaxing activities like reading a book, listening to calming music, or practicing meditation. This can help to quiet your mind and ease you into sleep.

Light Stretching

Gentle stretching can relieve tension in your body and promote relaxation. Avoid strenuous workouts as they can make it difficult to fall asleep. Light stretching can be a good way to unwind before bed.

Exercise Regularly but Not Too Close to Bedtime

1. Morning or Afternoon Workouts

Regular physical activity promotes better sleep, but avoid intense workouts too close to bedtime. Schedule your exercise sessions earlier in the day, leaving ample time for your body to cool down before sleep.

2. Benefits of Exercise

Exercise helps regulate your circadian rhythm, improves sleep quality, and reduces stress levels. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week.

3. Light Stretching Before Bed

Gentle stretching before bed can relax your muscles and promote relaxation. However, avoid vigorous exercise or intense workouts as they can interfere with your sleep.
